About Us

We are a family-run guesthouse located in the heart of Kyrgyzstan. Our mission is to provide a warm and welcoming environment for all our guests. Our guesthouse is surrounded by stunning mountains and beautiful landscapes, making it the perfect place to relax and unwind.

About Kyrgyzstan

Kyrgyzstan is a country located in Central Asia, known for its stunning natural beauty, rich culture, and warm hospitality. The country is home to the majestic Tien Shan mountains, pristine lakes, and vast steppes, making it a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts and nature lovers. Kyrgyzstan is also known for its rich cultural heritage, with a blend of nomadic traditions and modern influences. Visitors can experience traditional Kyrgyz hospitality, sample delicious local cuisine, and explore the vibrant culture of this unique country.

Why choose this destination

Kyrgyzstan offers a unique blend of adventure and tranquility. Whether you're hiking in the Tien Shan mountains, horseback riding across vast steppes, or exploring UNESCO World Heritage sites like the ancient Silk Road cities of Osh and Bishkek, this destination has something for everyone. Experience warm Kyrgyz hospitality and breathtaking scenery at Guesthouse Terrabella.
